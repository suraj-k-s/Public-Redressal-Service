    Public Grievance Portal is an online platform available to the citizens regarding to the Department of Public Grievances.
    Redressing the citizen's grievances, is one of the most important initiatives of the department the website to lodge their grievances to the public authorities on any subject related to service delivery. The citizens can enter all the mandatory details in the form provided in the Lodge Grievance page and submit the form.Citizens can attach any document that support their matter. All the details should be correct and it must me confirmed before submitting the form. The visitors are also required to enter the captch for security reasons. After the successfull submission the applicant can track the grievance using the provided in the mail. Feel free to add the feedback in the feedback page. The contact information is also provided.


    The status of the grievance filed in CPGRAMS can be tracked with the unique registration ID.
    The following matters will not be considered :
    -> Personal matters
    -> Matters that offend the governement or any other Department in any way.
    -> No suggestion are accepted
    -> Fake documents
